What do we do?

Simply put, we play Dungeons & Dragons regularly as a group. But we don't leave it at that. We pair it with great food, drinks and a pub atmosphere.

Our name

"A blood-orange sunrise crests the peaks of Rage Mountain, as the eight of you arrive at the troubled realm of Galindor" - Abed

As the fictional set of the second D&D episode in Community, we felt Galindor captures all of the fun as well as the sometimes high stakes a game of D&D can bring.
